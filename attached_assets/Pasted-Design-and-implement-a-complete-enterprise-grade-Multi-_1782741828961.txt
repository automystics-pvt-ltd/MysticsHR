Design and implement a complete enterprise-grade **Multi-Tenant SaaS Platform** with centralised Platform Admin, Tenant Admin, Subscription Management, Billing, User Management, and Self-Service Customer Onboarding.

### Customer Self-Service Portal

* Allow customers to Sign Up directly from the application login page.
* Support email/OTP verification and secure account activation.
* Automatically create a new tenant after successful registration.
* Guide customers through subscription selection, payment, and tenant setup.
* Allow customers to log in immediately after successful activation.
* Support Forgot Password, Reset Password, Change Password, and MFA.

### Subscription & Payment

* Complete SaaS subscription lifecycle management.
* Monthly, Yearly, Trial, Enterprise, and Custom plans.
* Support plan upgrades, downgrades, renewals, renew-before-expiry, cancellations, and grace periods.
* Integrate secure payment gateways supporting Credit/Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking, QR Code payments, and Wallets.
* Automatically generate invoices, payment receipts, GST invoices, and payment confirmations.
* Immediately activate or upgrade tenant subscriptions after successful payment.
* Automatically remove payment warnings and unlock subscribed modules after payment.
* Maintain complete payment history, transaction logs, receipts, subscription history, and billing reports.
* Provide dedicated Subscription Management and Plan Upgrade screens for customers.

### Platform Admin Portal

* Manage the complete SaaS platform from a centralized dashboard.
* Automatically receive every newly registered tenant.
* View tenant profile, subscription plan, payment status, invoices, receipts, user count, storage usage, module usage, renewal status, and login activity.
* Create, edit, activate, suspend, archive, enable/disable, or delete tenants.
* Configure user limits, storage, branches, warehouses, API usage, feature access, and modules per tenant.
* Monitor tenant health, license utilization, analytics, audit logs, and platform performance.

### Tenant Administration

* Tenant Admin can create, edit, activate/deactivate, and manage users directly from the application.
* Allow admins to invite users or create accounts and let users securely create their own passwords.
* Support password reset, account recovery, MFA, session management, departments, approval hierarchy, and RBAC.
* Prevent user creation beyond licensed limits with meaningful validation.

### Role-Based Access Control

* Dynamic RBAC across all modules.
* Automatically register new modules in Roles & Permissions.
* Control menus, pages, dashboards, APIs, reports, actions, approvals, and data visibility based on assigned roles.

### Security

* Highly secured Platform Admin Portal accessible only to authorized Platform Administrators.
* Strong authentication, MFA, session timeout, IP restrictions (optional), audit logging, encryption, secure APIs, tenant isolation, and complete activity tracking.
* Prevent unauthorized access to Platform Admin features and tenant data.

### Performance & Reliability

* Ensure all modules work without functional, logical, or deployment issues.
* Optimize APIs, database queries, caching, lazy loading, background processing, and large data handling.
* Production-safe deployments with no routing, build, permission, or 404 issues.

### User Experience

* Premium enterprise-grade UI/UX.
* Fast, responsive, intuitive workflows.
* Mobile, tablet, desktop, and web compatibility.
* Consistent dashboards, search, filters, pagination, notifications, and seamless navigation.

The entire SaaS platform must be production-ready, secure, scalable, configurable, high-performance, and capable of supporting future growth without architectural changes, while providing a seamless self-service onboarding and subscription experience for customers and complete operational control for Platform Administrators.
