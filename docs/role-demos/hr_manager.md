## Suggested demo flow

1. Create a new employee at `/employees/new`; the onboarding checklist auto-generates.
2. Approve the seeded leave request at `/leave/approvals` and show the calendar update.
3. Walk through the active cycle at `/performance`, drill into goals and appraisals.
4. Send an announcement from `/communications`.
